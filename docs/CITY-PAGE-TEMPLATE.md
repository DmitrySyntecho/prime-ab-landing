# CITY PAGE TEMPLATE & CONTENT STRUCTURE

## Overview

City pages are location-specific landing pages designed to capture local search intent and drive leads from major US cities. Each city page follows a standardized template with 11 required sections while maintaining unique, locally-relevant content that prevents duplicate page issues.

---

## URL STRUCTURE & HIERARCHY

### Format
```
/locations/[state]/[city]-av-production
```

### Examples
- `/locations/florida/miami-av-production`
- `/locations/florida/orlando-av-production`
- `/locations/california/los-angeles-av-production`
- `/locations/california/san-diego-av-production`

### Search Intent Captured
- "AV production Miami"
- "LED wall rental Miami"
- "Event production Miami"
- "Sound system rental Miami"
- "[City] event production services"

## SECTION 4: LOCAL EVENT PRODUCTION CONTEXT

**Purpose:** Make the page locally unique by including venue references, production considerations, and market expertise

**Required Content Topics:**

### Popular Venues
List 3-5 actual venues frequently used in the city:
- Convention centers
- Hotels & resorts
- Theaters
- Event spaces
- Outdoor venues

Example:
```
In Miami we frequently support events at:
- Miami Beach Convention Center
- Fontainebleau Miami Beach
- JW Marriott Marquis Miami
- Pérez Art Museum Miami
- Wynwood Walls (outdoor)
```

### Event Districts & Production Hubs
- Identify key areas known for events/entertainment
- Explain why these areas require specific AV solutions

### Local Logistics Considerations
- Venue access and load-in requirements
- Parking and transportation logistics
- Climate/weather considerations
- Specific technical requirements common in the market

### Market Production Experience
- Years of experience in the specific city
- Notable clients or event types served
- Local relationships with venues/vendors

**Linking Requirements:**
- Internal links to resource pages about venue planning (optional)
- No required external links in this section

**SEO Purpose:**
- Creates unique, locally-relevant content that cannot be duplicated
- Establishes authority in the specific market
- Improves local search relevance signals

**CRITICAL RULE:**
Each city page MUST include unique venue names, logistics information, and market context. Do NOT generate generic city pages with only city names changed.

## INTERNAL LINKING REQUIREMENTS SUMMARY

Every city page must include the following minimum internal links:

| Link Type | Minimum | Target Pages |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Service Pages | 3 | `/services/*` |
| Industry Pages | 2 | `/industries/*` |
| Resource Pages | 2 | `/resources/*` |
| Nearby City Pages | 3-5 | `/locations/[state]/*` |
| **Total Minimum** | **10-12** | Various |

### Linking Best Practices
- Use descriptive anchor text (e.g., "LED wall rental" not "click here")
- Distribute links naturally throughout the page
- Avoid excessive linking in single sections
- Link strategically to pages with lower internal link authority first

## CONTENT UNIQUENESS RULES

### MANDATORY UNIQUE CONTENT

Each city page MUST include unique content that makes it different from other city pages:

1. **Venue Names & Details**
   - Must include actual venue names specific to the city
   - Cannot be generic or interchangeable between cities

2. **Local Logistics Notes**
   - Must address city-specific challenges, parking, load-in procedures
   - Cannot be copied between cities

3. **Market-Specific Event Types**
   - Emphasize the event categories most common in that market
   - For example: Miami might emphasize fashion/music events; Silicon Valley emphasizes tech conferences

4. **Regional Production Considerations**
   - Climate, seasonal factors, local regulations
   - Venue-specific technical requirements

### PROHIBITED DUPLICATE PATTERNS

**DO NOT** create city pages with only the city name changed:
- ❌ "AV Production in [City]" with identical copy below
- ❌ Duplicating venue lists without verification
- ❌ Using generic event types not relevant to the market
- ❌ Copying logistics sections without local customization

**DO** ensure each page is manually researched and written with genuine local expertise:
- ✅ Verify venues actually exist and frequently host events
- ✅ Include logistics information specific to each market
- ✅ Emphasize event types prominent in that area
- ✅ Include market-specific production notes

## SUMMARY

City pages are a critical component of Prime Line AV's SEO strategy for capturing local search intent across the US. By following this template with mandatory local customization and strategic internal linking, the site can scale to 100+ city pages while maintaining content quality and avoiding duplicate content penalties.

Each page must balance:
- **Standardization** (11 required sections, consistent structure)
