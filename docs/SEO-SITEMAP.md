## KEYWORD CLUSTERING RULES

### NO Duplicate Keyword Targeting:
- ❌ Do NOT create /av-rental (conflicts with /services/av-production)
- ❌ Do NOT create /audio-visual-services (conflicts with /services/av-production)
- ❌ Do NOT create /av-company (conflicts with homepage)
- ❌ Do NOT create separate rental vs production pages (consolidate under services)

### Keyword Consolidation:
All variations of "AV production", "audio visual production", "AV rental company" → **/services/av-production**

All location variants like "AV production in Florida", "AV services Florida" → **/locations/florida-av-production**

All equipment rentals → **/services/{service-name}** + **/equipment/{product-category}**

## CONTENT STRATEGY NOTES

1. **Service pages are the SEO engine** - they target the highest commercial intent and receive most internal links
2. **Location pages provide geographic distribution** - enables "AV production in [city]" rankings nationwide
3. **Resource pages support AI visibility** - answer planning questions to appear in AI search results
4. **Equipment pages are supplementary** - support service pages and calculators, not primary traffic drivers
5. **All pages funnel to conversion tools** - calculators and packages drive quote requests
